    Litcafe

    In cities full of large malls and big restaurants and in a society where the paradigm of large reigns our imagination, sometimes that small little café in one corner of the road with just 4 tables catches my attention. It is that small cozy place which is frequented by a small group of people. The owner knows everyone and there is a comfortable sense of belonging to that place which serves as a retreat from everything big and extravagant. Do you relate to that kind of a place? Well LitCafe at Felicity is exactly that kind of a place. In a fest that is alive with a myriad events, a small group of people gather to stimulate their creative centers and have a good time. From creative writing to speaking, there are events to suit everyone’s tastes.

    Kalakshetra

    Art has since time immemorial provided for unparalleled expression of humanity's best. We celebrate that with our own Art mini-fest called Kalakshetra. Felicity hosts a warm and cozy set of events for everyone who is ready to paint the town red and perhaps get a little paint on themselves too! Some of the events are

    • Pot painting

      Indulge in the traditional art of pot painting with others to create a colourful display.

    • Paper Dressing

      Realise your dream to be a princess or a warrior using newspaper along with loads of other.

    • Face painting

      Participate in Felicity's own spin of Halloween. Mask your face in paint and wear a different identity.

    • Grafiti

      Street Artists and followers of the hippie culture have always loved signing walls with a bit of themselves. Paint our walls with your creativity and leave your mark on our walls and hearts.

    • T-Shirt Design

      Bring your hip T-Shirt design to life and win prizes.

    Carnival

    Take a step back in time and experience the amazing Indian Mela as Felicity puts up a carnival for those who crave the Indian flavour. The carnival will house stalls with games and food and a lot of euphoria. The Carnival Arena will have a variety of stalls with exciting party games to play and win coupons. These coupons would be redeemable at food stalls which will serve food of all major cuisines.

About

What's it all about!

When a campus full of a myriad of people and ideas reaches a critical mass, the latent energy awakens to manifest itself into a grandeur.
Felicity is the annual techno-cultural fest of IIIT-H.
The students of the institute come together to create an event that is the expression of their creativity and technical prowess.
Come experience a zestful three day long bonanza that has something for everyone!
