RULES :
1. At every level, 5 questions need to be answered.

2. Every question has a maximum of 50 points.

3. The answers can be one word or a simple phrase.

4. Based on the time to answer a question the points scored can vary.

6. Hints will be provided from time to time.

7. The participants will be ranked first on number of questions solved. In case of a tie, the time taken to solve the questions will be considered.
