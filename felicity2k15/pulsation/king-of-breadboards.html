King Of Bread Boards

Pen it .Wire it. Spark it.

Ever wondered about the small green board in your laptop ? Pretty intriguing right ? Ever wondered where it all started ?

Show us how creative you can get with chips and connections on a breadboard. This contest involves solving of intricate problems in the simplest way, using existing circuits and available chips.

For all you circuit designers out there, test your designing speed and build prototype models here at Felicity.

RULES:

1 Contestants are allowed only in teams of two.

2. Contestants must be present at the venue at least 15 minutes before the start of the event .

3. Problem statements would require a minimum amount of logical thinking and an idea about the basic electronics components

4. Problems will be assigned in a random manner, exchange of questions once awarded is highly discouraged .

5. All the components required for the implementation would be given at the venue .
