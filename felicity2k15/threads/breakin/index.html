Break In CTF

Break & Get In

The official Capture The Flag event for Felicity Threads. Break In is a jeopardy-style CTF event, where you use whatever means at your disposal to win. If you enjoy finding ways in which a system can be broken, this event is for you.

Scoreboard

Rules:

Teams :


  1. A team, as defined as a collaborative group of individuals, is only allowed to compete under one team name.

  2. A team is not allowed to give themselves or another team an unfair advantage.

  3. A member of one team is not allowed to share answers with another team. We WILL find out if you do.

  4. More than one uniquely named teams working together to secure multiple final round spots will not be tolerated. In this case, both teams will be disqualified.

  5. Register yourself on felicity to form teams.


Competition:

  1. In one team, there is a maximum limit of 2 players(strictly) .

  2. CTF will be of 12 hrs. It can be changed if need be.

  3. Prizes worth Rs 15000.



Infrastructures & Flags:

  1. Please do not attack any BreakInCTF infrastructure.

  2. We would give extra points to the teams who come forward about the potential mistakes in our Infrastructure.

  3. Denial of service attacks will not be tolerated and the team responsible for any kind of DDOS will be terminated immediately.

  4. irc Channel : #breakin
