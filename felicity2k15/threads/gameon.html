Nice Gesture

Develop a gesture recognition based game that does not use hand gestures. The human forearm from the shoulder to fingers is considered as hand.

Rules:

  • Participants must be registered students of a university or college

  • The participants may submit as individuals or as teams

  • Multiple submissions are allowed

  • The submitter must provide a presentation explaining gameplay

  • The submitted game must be playable by persons who are not a part of the development team

  • Use of any input devices like kinect, webcam, mobile phone, etc and output devices such as mobile phone, monitor, projector etc are permitted

  • The developers may use any suitable coding framework and language

  • The developers can retain complete copyrights to the game

  • Developers must document and present their development process through a blog or buildinprogress.media.mit.edu

  • Register before 10/2/2015 11:59 PM

  • You can also submit by mailing every detail of your game on gameon@felicity.iiit.ac.in

Evaluation criterion:


    A user group with fixed number of players that includes Judges and participants (excluding the developers of the game) will provide marks out of 10 points for each of the following criteria:

  • background visual elements

  • foreground visual elements

  • animated visual elements

  • background sound track

  • audio elements used in gameplay

  • engaging gameplay

  • ease of navigation and user interaction (redo, save, view map, etc)

  • bug free performance
