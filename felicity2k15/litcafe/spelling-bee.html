Spell Bee

There will be more than just a B to spell

Are you a logophile? (If you did not have to google that word, this is just the write contest for you - pun intended. If you did, well then even better :D )
Are you that annoying grammar nazi in your group, the one who can not live without correcting spellings? Or even if you're just looking for some fun, LitCafe Felicity brings to you the Spelling Bee Contest. Join us and we'll show you why spellings can be fun, and how even the most cold hearted error pointers will have to rack their brains and chew on their pencils on the battle field, and maybe your heart will melt for them. Wear your language armors, we're coming for you.
